Chrysopoeia Evolve 1

Duality—possessing and expressing two states; a Janus head, looking forward and back. Much like the concept of the homunculus, the tiny life form born of the primordial ooze, chrysopoeia refers to a generative process, one that is conceived by the merging and mutual metamorphosis of disparate ideas.

Essay, photographs, book. Shown at Investigating the Lab, RISD Design Science Symposium, 2014 and at Biomimicry, Brown University Science Center, 2015.
