Biomaterial Production

As a member of the 2014 Stanford–Brown–Spelman iGEM team, my team and I experimented with both synthetic-biological and common industrial biomaterials. We explored molding and shaping starch plastics, cellulose-based bioplastics, and other natural polymer materials. Ultimately, we built a rough prototype for a biologically-produced, biodegradable unmanned aerial vehicle. Our project and my work received press in Forbes, Wired, New Scientist, and many other publications.

Synthetic biology, bioplastics, molds.
